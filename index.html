The Spirit of Eastern Art

Eastern art is not merely visual pleasure—it is spiritual practice. It seeks "yi jing" (artistic conception) rather than literal representation, creating infinite resonance within limited space. Every stroke carries the artist's breath; every silence speaks volumes.
